## Theme rationale

v0.9.5 (2026-05-18) shipped three explicit deferrals to v0.9.6: WORM
store-side enforcement (P3.2 follow-up), CLI RBAC mirror (P3.3
follow-up), and federal-SI carry-overs surfaced by the AI-persona
walk-through validation. The v0.9.5 Q3 2026 quarterly resync of
`docs/positioning-and-value.md` (line 1159) added three new material
recommendations: **CONMON MCP first-mover** (verified-unclaimed),
**OSCAL 1.1.2 → 1.2.1 upgrade**, and a **positioning shift** toward
"deterministic, auditable, OSS-native compliance engineering"
counter-positioning the Vanta / Drata / Optro / Optro-Midship
"agentic GRC" convergence.

The federal-SI walk-through is deferred to v0.9.7 (Allen's
2026-05-18 scope lock-in) because no real-operator reviewer is
sourced yet. The v0.9.5 AI-persona validation remains the current
baseline; v0.9.6 ships the FIPS 199 + OMB + SCR + WORM + RBAC
+ CONMON MCP surfaces so they are ready when the walk-through
happens.
