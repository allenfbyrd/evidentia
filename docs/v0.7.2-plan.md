## Theme

**"Supply-chain polish + documentation refresh."**

v0.7.1 closed the AI features enterprise-grade gap (typed exception
hierarchy, `@with_retry` for transient LLM failures, `GenerationContext`
metadata, ECS structured logging, and audit-trail correlation across
`risk_statements/` and `explain/`). v0.7.2 picks up the
**deferred-from-v0.7.1** items — small, high-signal supply-chain
hardening + documentation refresh — without expanding scope into the
v0.8+ "OSS-native AI moat" features.

This is, like v0.7.1, a deliberately scoped polish release. The v0.8+
direction (DFAH determinism harness, MCP server, DSE screenshot
validator, etc. — see [positioning-and-value.md §13.2](positioning-and-value.md))
remains out of scope and will get its own focused cycle.

## Origin of this plan

This plan was created at v0.7.1 ship time (2026-04-26) by **moving the
deferred items** out of `docs/v0.7.1-plan.md` into a forward-looking
v0.7.2 doc. The v0.7.1 release shipped as **P0-only** (AI features
hardening) per the user's deliberate scope-narrowing decision after the
P0 work completed in 8 commits during the April 2026 development window.
The supply-chain polish + documentation refresh items, originally
scoped for the same v0.7.1 release, were judged better-served by their
own focused cycle than by being rushed into v0.7.1.

The cross-link from `docs/v0.7.1-plan.md` "Deferred to v0.7.2" section
points here so anyone reviewing the v0.7.1 ship narrative can find the
forward plan in one click.
